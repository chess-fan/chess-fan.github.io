Welcome to ChessCoin!

About

Our coin started in 2016.
It uses the POS (proof of stake) mechanism to create blocks. The annual staking rate is 32%.
ChessCoin allows instant transaction processing with 64 second blocks, 2 hours minimum stake time and 32 blocks coin maturity.

FAQ


How to get your wallet started?

1. Copy the chesscoin.conf file into the correct directory, e.g.

  • Windows: C:\Users\*.*\AppData\Roaming\ChessCoin
  • Linux: /home/yourOwnUserHere/.chesscoin
2. and then start your wallet.

The rules:
  • We play 3:0 games, that means: each player gets 3 minutes and no time increment. One tournament is set to 2h.
  • The points from both days count: to motivate people joining, the final point table includes the points you made for both tournaments.
    A win gives you 2 points, draw: 1 point.
    There is also "berserk mode", and:
    also when you win consecutive games you get more points (e.g. you have won 2 games which gave you 4 points. When you now win also the 3rd game you get 4 points, so in total: 8 points)
  • The top3 winners overall - from both days - get a prize (how much? only top3?)

if we just use their sites for playing and make the prize distribution elsewhere, they can't complain.

what I wrote above is not the Holy Grail, and requires your feedback. We might iron out things more (e.g. dates/times; who gets how much prize, ...).
